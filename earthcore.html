Brand Story

Built Around Real Student Life

EarthCore started when I realised that traditional home gyms and memberships didn’t match how students and young professionals actually live — limited space, time and money. The brand is my answer: a compact, sustainable ecosystem that lets you train seriously from your own room.

Founder Story

Built From My Own Need for a Better Way to Train

As a full-time student, I experienced the exact problems EarthCore now solves: expensive memberships, crowded gyms, and non-eco-friendly equipment that didn’t fit my values or my room.

Kailash Ghorpade
Founder & Concept Designer, EarthCore Fitness

I created EarthCore to give students and young professionals like me a compact, sustainable way to stay strong. The brand is a blend of my own fitness journey, my interest in sustainable design, and my belief that training should fit real life—not the other way around.
